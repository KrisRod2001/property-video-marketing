The Video Editing Process

The process begins with uploading raw clips in the desired order, as they appear in the final video based on thumbnail arrangement. Editors trim shaky footage, adjust clip lengths for stability, and add titles (street number and name only, appearing 5 seconds in, fading after 4). Pre-roll and post-roll clips for branding use hard cuts or crossfades.

Music selection is critical—upload royalty-free tracks to match the tone. Notes for specific edits (e.g., excluding objects) ensure precision. AI tools enhance stabilization and color grading, while human editors refine pacing. Turnaround is typically 24-48 hours, with revisions available.
